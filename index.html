From Chaos to Control

MY_UnifiedCi directly addresses the critical challenges hindering our development velocity and quality. This section outlines the problems we face today and how our centralized solution provides a clear, effective path forward.

‚ùå The Problems: Current State

  • Pipeline Duplication: 50+ inconsistent, copy-pasted pipeline variations across teams.
  • Maintenance Nightmare: Updates require manually changing dozens of individual pipelines, causing errors.
  • Slow Onboarding: New projects take 2-3 days to configure CI/CD, wasting developer time.
  • Quality Inconsistency: Teams skip vital steps like testing or linting; reporting is non-standard.

‚úÖ The Solution: MY_UnifiedCi

  • Centralized Templates: One source of truth for all pipelines, ensuring consistency.
  • Effortless Maintenance: Update the central library once to apply changes everywhere.
  • Rapid Onboarding: New projects are CI/CD-ready in 5 minutes with a single line of code.
  • Enforced Best Practices: Quality gates, testing, and standardized reporting are built-in.
